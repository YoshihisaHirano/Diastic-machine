Mac Low Diastic Generator

This is a text generator based on the idea by artist/poet Jackson Mac Low.
A word ("seed") is put in the generator then it creates a phrase with each word in it having a letter at the same position as in the initial word. Read more about the creator.

Here's a quick example with the seed word "son". Its 1st letter is "s", 2nd is "o" and 3rd is "n". Depending on the text from which the words are extracted, the outcome can vary, but the result might look like this: "Sparrow lost pennies". Each word in the phrase has one letter at the same index as the seed. Hide the example
